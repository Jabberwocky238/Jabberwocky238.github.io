I concur with Andrew's point that good leaders are good communicators.

Leadership involves not only ==making informed decisions== but also ==inspiring, motivating, and guiding a team of diverse individuals.==

==Effective communication is essential for conveying a clear vision, setting expectations, and fostering collaboration.==

Leaders need to articulate their ideas, provide feedback, and resolve conflicts efficiently, which can only be achieved by people with good communication skills.

While a strong technical background is valuable, it does not necessarily equate to effective leadership.

Technical expertise can be essential for understanding the industry, but without the ability to communicate and lead effectively, it may be challenging to ==harness the collective potential of a team== or ==adapt to changing business environments==.
